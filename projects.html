Furniture volume and weight prediction

Technology: Python, Tensorflow
This project is an API allowing to have, by giving photos of rooms, an estimation of the total weight and volume of the pieces of furniture present in it.
The main component of this system is the detection and localization of furniture, allowing an in-depth study of each of them.
The system is served via a RESTful API realized with FastAPI.
Made during my internship at Bial-X.

Football matches bet maker

Technology: Python, Tensorflow
Website allowing the user to have recommendations of bets allowing a maximum profit expectation.
It is based on the prediction of the match result thanks to an ElasticNet developed with Tensorflow.
Predicted win probabilities are compared to the associated odds to determine whether a bet is attractive or not.
Made in collaboration with V. Grandjean and H. Marguet.

Quadratic Assignement Problem

Technology: Python
The quadratic assignment problem (QAP) is one of the fundamental combinatorial optimization problems in the branch of optimization or operations research in mathematics, from the category of the facilities location problems.
We tried 3 algorithms on it, with various results: a Tabu search, a Simulated Annealing algorithm and a genetic algorithm.
Made in collaboration with V. Grandjean.

Kohonen Maps

Technology: Python
Self-organising maps are a kind of Neural Network trained with unsupervised learning, to produce low dimensional representation of the input space, preserving its topological features.
We explored their properties on various datasets.
